Introduction¶

TMDb movie dataset contains information about 10,000 movies collected from The Movie Database (TMDb), including user ratings and revenue.

As a result of the investigation we'll try to gain information about different genres of the movies, rate their level of popularity, calculate their income and get more familiar with the features that are associated with different kinds of movies.
